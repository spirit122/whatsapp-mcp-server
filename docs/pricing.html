FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through Lemonsqueezy at any time. Your API key stays active until the end of the billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal via Lemonsqueezy.

Do I need my own WhatsApp Business account?

No. All plans include access to a shared test phone number. Enterprise includes setup of your own business number.

What if I lose my API key?

Check your Lemonsqueezy purchase email, or verify at: /billing/api-key?email=YOUR_EMAIL

Is there a free trial for Pro?

The free tier with 5 tools serves as your trial. Upgrade when you need webhooks, analytics, or interactive messages.

Can I self-host instead?

Yes! The code is open source (MIT). See the Self-Hosting guide.
